## Scope Snapshot
- Problem: The BA team across GEX1–GEX4 relies on manual Google Sheets/Forms for weekly reporting, leading to data inconsistency, lack of real-time visibility, and no automated risk analysis.
- Goal: Build a centralised web-based system that automates weekly BA reporting, enforces RBAC, tracks resource allocation, and leverages AI to generate risk summaries and performance evaluations.
- In scope: Dashboard, Project Setup, Resource Setup, Weekly Report submission, AI-Generated Reports (progress summary, RED/YELLOW/GREEN evaluation, effort cross-check, GEX health summary), Google Workspace SSO, data export (Excel/CSV).
- Out of scope: Mobile native apps, public-facing portals, AI model training on company data, third-party PM tool integrations.
