# BA-kit Community Agent Guide

Use the repo-native skills in `skills/`.

Primary commands:

- `/ba-do`
- `/ba-start intake`
- `/ba-start backbone`
- `/ba-start stories`
- `/ba-start srs-lite`
- `/ba-next`
- `/ba-status`

Community scope is solo and non-commercial only.
Do not invent modules, collaboration, packaging, publishing, or client delivery workflows.
When a request is out of scope, point to `docs/comparison.md` and `docs/upgrade.md`.
