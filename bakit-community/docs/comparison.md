# Comparison

## BA-kit Community

Built for solo, non-commercial BA work.

- one operator
- simple markdown artifacts
- narrow workflow from intake to srs-lite
- good fit for learning, evaluation, and small internal analysis

## BA-kit Paid

Built for real delivery across people and stakeholders.

- team coordination
- governance and review flow
- packaging for handoff and delivery
- client work and commercial operations

The difference is not "free with fewer features." The products solve different jobs.

Choose Community when you are proving the method alone.

Choose paid when the work needs team accountability, governed delivery, or stakeholder-ready outputs.
